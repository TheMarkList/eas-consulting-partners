About

The Expert Corporate Brands Trust

With over two decades in retail construction management, Elias Abu Shanab has built a reputation that speaks for itself. He has guided some of America's most recognized brands through complex, high-stakes construction programs — delivering on scope, on schedule, and to exacting corporate standards.

Where most consultants manage projects, Elias manages outcomes. He understands the language of the boardroom and the reality of the jobsite — and operates with complete fluency in both. That rare duality is why brands return to EAS when the project matters most.
